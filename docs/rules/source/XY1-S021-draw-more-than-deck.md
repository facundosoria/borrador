# XY1-S021 - Draw More Than Deck

```
WHAT IF YOU SHOULD DRAW MORE CARDS THAN YOU HAVE?
If a card tells you to draw or look at more cards from your deck than you have left, draw or look at the cards you have and continue
play as normal.
For example, if a card tells you to draw or look at the top 5 cards of your deck and you have only 3 cards left in your deck, draw the
top 3 or look at the top 3. You lose if you cannot draw a card at the start of your turn, but not if you cannot draw one because a card
told you to do so.
```
