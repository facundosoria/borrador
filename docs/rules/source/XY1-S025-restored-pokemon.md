# XY1-S025 - Restored Pokemon

```
APPENDIX C: RESTORED POKÉMON
Fossil Pokémon have their own special Stage: Restored Pokémon.
If you have a Restored Pokémon in your hand, you cannot play it to your Bench unless you play an Item card and its effect tells you to.
Restored Pokémon are not Basic Pokémon! Thus, you cannot play them as your Active or Benched Pokémon during setup.
Also, you must still have at least one Basic Pokémon in your deck if you otherwise only have Restored Pokémon in your deck.
Restored Pokémon are not Evolution cards.
Cards or effects that refer to “Basic,” “Stage 1,” “Stage 2,” or “Evolution” cards don’t affect Restored Pokémon.
When playing with older Fossil Pokémon, follow the “Evolves from” or “Put this card into play…” text.
```
