# XY1-S012 - Turn Actions

```
TURN ACTIONS
DRAW A CARD
Start your turn by drawing a card. (If there are no cards in your
deck at the beginning of your turn and you cannot draw a card,
the game is over, and your opponent wins.)
DO ANY OF THESE IN ANY ORDER:
Put Basic Pokémon cards from your hand onto your Bench (as many as you want).
Your Bench can hold up to 5 Pokémon.
Evolve Pokémon (as many as you want).
You may evolve a Pokémon that was in play at the start of your turn.
Neither player can evolve a Pokémon on its first turn in play.
You can’t evolve a Pokémon twice in the same turn.
Attach an Energy card to one of your Pokémon (once per turn).
Play Trainer cards (as many as you want, but only one Supporter card and one Stadium card per turn).
Retreat your Active Pokémon (once per turn).
To retreat, discard 1 Energy from your Active Pokémon for each Energy in its Retreat Cost.
Asleep or Paralyzed Pokémon cannot retreat.
Use Abilities (as many as you want).
Abilities aren’t attacks, so you can still attack if you use an Ability.
ATTACK AND END YOUR TURN
Once you attack, your turn is over.
On the first turn of the game, the starting player skips the attack step.
```
