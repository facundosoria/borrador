# XY1-S007 - Zones of the Pokémon TCG

```
Zones: Hand, Prize Cards, Deck, Discard Pile, Active, Bench, In Play.
Each player starts with and must always have one Active Pokémon.
Each player may have up to 5 Pokémon on the Bench.
Decks are 60 cards, order is hidden unless a card allows otherwise.
Prize cards are 6 cards set aside face down; when you KO, you take one of your own Prize cards.
If you take your last Prize card, you win.
```
