# XY1-S023 - Mega Evolution

```
SPECIAL RULES FOR MEGA EVOLUTION POKÉMON
The M is part of a Mega Evolution Pokémon’s name.
Mega Venusaur-EX evolves from Venusaur-EX (not Venusaur).
Mega Evolution Pokémon count as Evolution cards and follow normal Evolution rules.
When a Mega Evolution Pokémon is Knocked Out, your opponent takes 2 Prize cards.
When one of your Pokémon becomes a Mega Evolution Pokémon, your turn ends.
```
